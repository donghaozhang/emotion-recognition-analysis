🔍 Key Insights

📸 Multi-Modal Data
Combines facial images, text descriptions, and structured predictions with comprehensive metadata including multiple bounding box formats.
🧠 Rich Model Comparison
Features 9 different AI models including EXPW fine-tuned variants and leading vision-language models for comprehensive benchmarking.
🎯 Structured Reasoning
Model responses include structured thinking traces with <think> and <answer> tags, providing insight into AI reasoning processes.
📐 Precise Face Detection
Multiple coordinate systems and relative area measurements enable detailed face detection accuracy analysis.

Complete dataset with consistent labeling, high model coverage (95.4-100%), and balanced emotion distribution for research applications.

All expression labels are consistent with ground truth annotations, ensuring data integrity for reliable analysis results.
